Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process.

1

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but most website projects take between 4-8 weeks from initial consultation to launch. More complex e-commerce or web applications may take 8-12 weeks.

2

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer various support packages to ensure your digital presence continues to perform optimally after launch. Our support plans include regular updates, security monitoring, and performance optimization.

3

What is your pricing structure?

We provide custom quotes based on project requirements. Contact us with your project details for a personalized estimate. We offer both fixed-price projects for well-defined scopes and hourly rates for ongoing work.

4

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! We've worked with clients across the globe and can accommodate different time zones and requirements.

5

What technologies do you work with?

We work with a wide range of modern technologies including HTML, Tailwind CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, Python, Django and many others. We choose the best technology stack for each project based on its specific requirements.

6

Do you provide hosting services?

Yes, we offer managed hosting solutions optimized for performance and security. We can also help you set up hosting on platforms of your choice if you prefer to manage it yourself.
